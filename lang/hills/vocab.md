# vocab
 ## adverbs/prepositions
* -GL- (adv) - down, below, beneath, inside, deeply
* -NGL- (adv) - up, above, outside, beyond, surprisingly
* -RX- 
    * (adv) now, currently
    * (cl.adv.) continuously, "is/was VERBing"
* -M- (prep.) - agent/topic/predicate of vi
    * "by" w/pseudopassives
    * "by"/"in"/"about" w/experientials/perception-based
    * ML-M- - glad about
* -RL- (prep.) - possessive, "by" w/verbal nouns
* -qN- (prep.) - to, towards (w/in.); to, for (indir. obj. marker, w/an.)
* motion/change-of-state
* -qN-LL- (prep./adv.) - to (here), in this direction
* -qN-RR- (prep./adv.) - to (there), in that direction
* -qN-GL- (prep.) - (down) to/towards, beneath
* -qN-NGL- (prep.) - (up) to/towards, on top of, above
* -qN-NGL-LL- (prep./adv.) - (down) to/towards here, 
* -hR- (prep.) - from, away from
    * motion/change-of-state
* -GM- (prep.) - at, in
    * stative
* -gR- (prep.) - beside, next to, on the side of
* -GM-GL- (prep.) - below, beneath, under
* -GM-NGL- (prep.) - on top of, above
* -qL- (prep./adv.) - against, contrary to
    * wrestling, pushing a rock, swimming upstream
* -NgM-
    * (cl.adv.) - and, next, then
    * (adv.) and, also, additionally
    * (prep.) with
* -GRx- (cl.adv.) - interrogative marker
* -LqMLr- (adv.) - old, formerly

## nouns
* rN (an.) - someone, anyone, they
* rM (in.) - some-/anything
* hLR (an.) - (adult) (hill)person
* hLN (an.) - community, responsibility, the 'buddy system'
* xNhN (in) - deed, undertaking, project, effort
* xNhN-M-xN-(VERB) - the act of VERBing
    * xNhN-M-xN-gL-GL-qN-qRX-rRRGL - "journey back to yon home below," returning to the eastern hills (as a political project) 
    * xNhN-qL-xLxRGR - "project against Nature," in which the hills folk see them selves perpetually engaged
    * xNhN-M-xN-hNM-qL-xLxRGR - "making against Nature" — originally/primarily civil engineering but also agronomy, chemistry/alchemy 
* dRN (in.) - agreement, concord, cooperation, amiability
* qLXLLX (in.) - illness, disease
* NLRN (in.) - threat, danger, problem
* qRX (in.) - home, house
    * GML-Rg-LL-qRX-RL-NM-NN - "our home is here," which can mean anything from "get with the times" to "suck it up"
* xLNhM (in.) - game, pastime, sport(s), athletics/athletic activity, music, dance, acting
    * no intended/expected/hoped for end result
    * even solo hobbies are xNhN, not nLNhM
* GNMMM (in.) - ball
* xLNhM-M-GNMMM - ballgame
    * often shortened just to (M-)GNMMM
    * (xLNhM-)RR-M-GNMMM - a traditional ballgame (generic)
    * XLNhM-RR-M-GNMMM-NgM-hLR-NM - the ballgame everyone plays
* gRRxR (an.) - gestational polyp
* xLxRGR (an.) - (personified/deified) nature
* GNR (in.) - delineated period of time
* GNR-RL-NLRN-GL (in.) - windy season
    * "danger below time"—in hills, hills folk spend more time above ground, allegedly to avoid worm mating season but mostly because it's nice out
* GNR-RL-NLRN-NGL (in.) - rainy season
    * "danger above time"—in hills, hills folk spend more time below ground, to avoid landslides, flash floods, avalanches, etc
* GNM (in.) - year
* GNML (in.) - calendar
* <a id="xLgL-GL"></a>xLgL-GL (in.) "below plant"
    * basically anything without leaves—fungus, lichen, moss
* xLgL-NGL (in.) "above plant"
    * trees, shrubs, flowers, etc. 
* qRM (in.) - area, field, plaza
    * physical space that can be apprehended at once
    * an atrium but not a hallway
    * mostly above-ground
* qRM-RL-xLgL-NGL - stand of trees, grove, garden
* qRM-LL-RL-G-L-xLgL-NGL-qRg - The Grove/The City
* qRM-LL-NGL - shadyside
* qRM-RR (derogatory)
* qRM-LL-GL - sunnyside
* hNNrN (in.) - meeting
* RhRxRLL (in.) - municipal government
    * specifically boughs folk government
    * original meaning "dealing with outsiders"
* qRM-LL-RL-RhRxRLL (in.) - Admin District
* qRM-LL-RL-hLR-LqMLr (in.) - Old Hill ("former hills folk district")
* qMLN (in.) - river, stream (moving body of water)
* qMLg (in.) - lake, sea (non-moving body of water)
* xRLL (an.) - boughs folk
* qRM-qLN-LL-RL-xRLL (in.) - The Underbrush
* qRM-LL-RL-xRLL (in.) - New Hill
* qRM-LL-RL-dRN (in.) - Harmony Plaza
* qRM-LL-RL-qMLN-GL (in.) - Market District
* qRM-LL-RL-qMLN-NGL (in.) - Long Dock
* qRM-LL-RL-xNhN (in.) - Barrel District
* qRM-rNL-NGL-LL (in.) - Low Quarter
* MMhMN (in.) - street, tunnel, hallway, canal
    * artificial passage
* qRLqR (in.) - base (metal), ore, copper
* qRLLN (in.) - additive, reagent, tin/zinc
    * also "secret sauce" (complementary)/"one weird trick" (derogatory)
* qRLhNR (in.) - alloy, combination, bronze, brass, synthesis
* hLLL (in.) - hair ornament, keepsake, trophy, accomplishment
* <a id="hLdN"></a>hLdN (in.) - food baked/cooked in containers
    * → plainsfolk _'eto_

## verbs
* GML (vi) - be at
* ML (vi) - agree (with), be suited (by), fit (with)
* ML-GL (vi) - be pleased by, love
* ML-NGL (vi) - unexpectedly enjoy, delight in, pleasingly discover
* qRL (vi) - smell (like) to, seem to
    * subject is smeller, thing that smells marked w/-M-
    * uncommon; qRLNGL more usual
* qRL-NGL (vi) - (unexpectedly/at first glance) seem to
* MxL (vt) - see, perceive
* vLML (vi) - appeal (to)
* gL (vi) - go
* gL-GL - go back (to)
* hNM (vt) - do, make
* hNN (vi) - be subject (to), suffer (of/from)
    * passivizer: `hNN-R-[-N-]-M-xN-(VERB)[-RL-(AGENT)][-N-](SUBJ)` = SUBJ is VERBed [by AGENT]
* xNM (vt) - be able to (do/perform)
    * + -xN-V - be able to, can
