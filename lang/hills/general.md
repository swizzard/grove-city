# hills
## phonology
* no pure vowels only syllablized sonorants
* less teeth shown = more polite
### consonants
 * "back"
     * /ɢ/, /q/, /h/ ("hard")
        * G, q, h
    * /ʁ/, /χ/ ("soft")
        * r, X
 * "front"
     * /v ~ f/, /ɖ ~ ʈ/ ("hard", uncommon)
        * f, d
    * /x/, /ɣ/ ("soft")
        * x, g
### vowels
* /m̩/, /n̩/, /ɬ̩/, /ɹ̩/
    * M, N, L, R
* /m̩n/, /m̩ɬ/, /m̩ɹ/, /ɬ̩ɹ/, /ɹ̩m/, /ɹ̩n/, /ɹ̩ɬ/
* length non-phonemic so everything gets same length leading to a bit of a droning quality
### syllables
* V, VV, CV, CVV, CVC, CVVC
* only "soft" consonants (/ʁ/, /χ/, /x/, /ɣ/) allowed in coda
* (C)V(V)C.C not licit w/in words
* V.VV, VV.V, VV.VV common

## syntax
* ergative-absolutive, animate/inanimate
    * animate marks for object/intrans. subj., inanimate marks for trans. subj
    * but also like adjectives &c marked f/animacy
* inclusive/exclusive 1pl
    * maybe ~inclusive/exclusive 2 instead of t/v
* so much deixis
    * here, there, yon beneath, yon above, yon other/unknown (most distal)
* agglutinative
* number unmarked
* definiteness marker
### verbs
* present, future, recent past, distant past
* indicative, irrealis, stative
* stative = habitual, aphoristic, implication of broad/obvious/unequivocal truth
* no future irrealis or future stative
* irrealis & (indicative) future used for questions
* hortative expressed lexically w/vi
* continuous indicated w/adverb
* verbal noun suffix
#### persons
* 1s/1p incl
* 1p exclusive
* 2s/2p inclusive
* 2s/2p 'exclusive'
* 3s/3p
* 2p 'exclusive' f/minors & non-hills folk
    * participant in coming of age ritual addressed w/2p exclusive before they swear to participate in xNhN-qL-xLxRGR

* no passive voice but vis corresponding to passive form of several common vts, plus a general "be subjected to" + verbal noun
* verb+deictic ~ german
* preceding tense marker
### "word" order
* `Vroot-Tense\[-Mood-\][-N-/-R-][CLAUSAL ADVERB]`
    * finite
        * vi: `V([cl.adv.][top.][indir. obj.][temp.][loc.][etc.]+)S`
        * vt: `VS([cl.adv.][indir. obj.][temp.][loc.][etc.]+)O`
    * non-finite follows but
        * vi subj marked -M- (anim & in.)
        * vi agent/topic/predicate still/also marked w/-M-
        * this means e.g. pseudopassives can be ambiguous
* vt subj marked -RL- (anim.) or -RL-R- (in.)
* `N(deic.)adj`
#### 'clausal adverb'
* and, interrogative marker
#### relative clauses
* separate words
* correlative
## functional words
### deictics
* LL - here, this
* RR - there, that
* rRRGL - yon below/beneath, that one under/beneath there, the previous/aforementioned
* rRRNGL - yon above/outside, that one out/up there
### persons
* -NN- - 1s/1p incl.
* -gN- - 2s
* -rN- 3p

* -xN- verbal noun marker
* -G- definiteness marker

* G-N-ADJ →superlative
### tense markers
* -R- present
* -LXL- - recent past
* -LqL- - distant past
### mood markers
* -∅- indicative
* -g- - stative
* -r- - irrealis
### case markers
* -N- - animate subject of intransitive verb/animate object of transitive verb
* vi: following verb & marking subject (V-N-...-N-SUBJ)
* vt: marking object (V-SUBJ-...-N-OBJ)
* -R- - inanimate subj of transitive verb
