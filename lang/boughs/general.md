# boughs

## phonology

### tones
* high
    * `´`
* low
    * <pre>`</pre>

* moving
    * `^`
    * rules
        * not allowed in monosyllabic words
        * starts at tone of previous syllable and moves to tone of following syllable
            * if it's the first syllable, it starts the opposite of the second syllable and ends the same as the second syllable
            * it's the last syllable, it starts as the same as the second-to-last syllable and ends as the opposite of the second-to-last syllable
