# vocab
## verbs
* wep
    * (vi) - be alert, look out, keep watch
    * (vt) - see, look at, look for
* mmattam
    * (vi) - be/stay silent, not speak
    * (vi) - refrain from doing/refuse to do
* 'ettin
    * (vt) - bury, store, put up
        * food &c, not bodies
        * also used for e.g. roasting among coals
* nnattin
    * (vt) - dig up, unwrap, open
* 'emmamm
    * (vt) - crush, grind, mash
* nnen
    * (vt) - have/make (someone else do something), get (something from someone or someone to do something)
        * not by force
        * possibly trickery but also just paying for something
* pemmam
    * (vt) - carry, bear
    * (vi) -  worry about, + 'ako- -tti

* nappo (adj) - shaded, covered
* ttokk (adj) - exposed, overt, uncovered

* nno'i (adj) - low, short, small
* nipi (adj) - tall, big
* mammo (adj.) - adult, mature
* nepo (adj.) - young
* mi'o (adj) - prominent, visible, large
* takita - crispy, cruncy
* mmakka - sweet
* kkatta - savory/umami
* ppa'a - spicy, hot
* tto'e - fallow, wintry, desolate
* towa - rainy, wet, cold, wintry
* kkott - dry, hot, summery
* kani - old
* 'akakow - 'akow-like, predatory
* ttewatti - literally "in (the) uniform"
    * appropriately-dressed, convincingly-dressed, well-dressed, handsome/beautiful
    * kids in church clothes (kkeppiko ttewa, etc)
    * dressed up for a date
        * complement coming from your bros, probably judgmental coming from your family, an insult coming from your date themselves
    * firefighter/sports/parade/military uniforms
    * fair, reasonable (of a price or terms)
    * neat, well-maintained (of a place)
    * resembling, imitating, "dressed as," pretending to be (thing being imitated + -ko)
        * insulting — unoriginality/copying is stigmatized
        * we'akow ke'ako ttewatti - "the 'akow is dressed as a person" →"'akow pretend to be people" →you can't trust some people
        * ke'ako ttewatti 'akow - "'akow dressed as a person," thief, 'biter'
