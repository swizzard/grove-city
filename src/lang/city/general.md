## "Low Hills"
* urban/youth variant
* less concern about showing teeth/opening mouth
### phonology
* emergence of 'inherent vowel' /ə ~ ʊ/
    * /ɢm̩/ → /ɢʊmː/
    * /xɹ̩ɬ̩/ →/xəɹʊɬ/, /xəɹʊw/, /xəɹʊː/
    * /hɬ̩ɣm̩/ →/hʊlɣʊm/, /ɂʊlɣʊm/
#### consonants
* /ɢ ~ g/
* /q ~ k/ 
* /ʁ ~ χ/ (usually unvoiced word-initially)
    * realization of /h/ as well as /ʁ/ and /χ/
* /ɣ ~ x/ (usually unvoiced word-initially)
* /w/ (realization of /β/, loanwords)
* /d/
* /m/, /n/, /ɬ ~ l/, /ɹ ~ r/ (non-phonemic)
#### vowels
* /ə ~ ʊ/
