## lexicon
* proper names CVCV
### functional words
#### classifiers
* _me_ - food, single-use things, spans of time
* _'e_ - places, ages, titles/honorifics, body parts 
* _ke_ - tall/narrow/solitary/vertically-oriented things, certain animals & plants, non-birthing people
* _te_ - broad/wide/dense/bushy things, certain animals & plants, birthing people
* _we_ - small/fast/bright things, certain animals & plants, children
* `CLS-'e` - pronominal
* _-'i-_ plural marker
